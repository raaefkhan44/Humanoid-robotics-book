## Glossary of Advanced Terms

- **Affordance**: The possibility of an action that a robot can perform with an object
- **Embodiment**: The physical form of a robot and its impact on intelligence
- **Morphological Computation**: Computation that emerges from the physical properties of a robot
- **Grounded Cognition**: Cognition that is shaped by physical interactions with the environment
